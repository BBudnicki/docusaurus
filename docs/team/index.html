The Team

Governance#

The Geneva Declaration on Citizen Science Data and Metadata Standards provides a consensus basis for the establishment of a Governance Committee for the PPSR-Core, as well as the initial membership of that Committee and some broad general principles for consideration of amendments to the PPSR-Core standard.
